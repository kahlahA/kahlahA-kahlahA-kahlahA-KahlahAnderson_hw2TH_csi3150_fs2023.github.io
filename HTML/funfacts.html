The Unwind series is a drama-packed chilling thriller that takes place in a not-so-distant future where the concept of a person's 'right to life' has taken a deeply chilling and perverted turn. Between the ages of 13 to 18, a legal guardian can sign away their parental rights to that of the government. The child isn't taken to a group home, but instead a facility where their own bodies belong to the state and its people, and would be given away as such. Meaning, if a person is in dire need of a new heart, transplanted limbs, or even new eyes, great news! You are the donor. Whether or not you are still using them makes no difference. And thanks to advancements in medical science, there is no need to worry about the recipient's body rejecting the implant.
